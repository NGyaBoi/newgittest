-= R.I.P. Florian Schneider=-

By Neel Ghosh (NGyaBoi)


It is the 11th of May as of writing this...
and the news came in a few days ago that Florian Schneider of Kraftwerk fame has past away.
This news really hit close to home for me.
I found Kraftwerk's music in my first year of high school.
Their music was a real shockaroo for me at the time.
I was just getting started with developing my own musical tastes.
Prior to this, I really only listened to surface level indie music and some Top 40 stuff.
My initial foray into weird electronic music was Aphex Twin, but that's a story for another time.
With my discovery of Kraftwerk, I started to appreciate the progression of technology and how that shaped music.
I used to listen to the song Autobahn on the bus ride to school.
That song perfectly fit both the timeslot of the bus ride.
It also fits very well the mood of a drive in the morning, with its sunny synth melodies and repetition.

So I just wanna give a quick shoutout to Kraftwerk for all the inspiring music they have put out.
R.I.P. Florian.
